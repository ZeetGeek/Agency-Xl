Installation Instructions

Follow these simple steps to install the Agency XI Figma template and start customizing your digital agency website design.

Extract the Agency XI File

  • Locate the downloaded Agency XI template file on your computer.

  • Right-click on the file and select "Extract" or "Unzip" to extract its contents.

Open the Agency XI Folder > Figma Folder

  • Navigate to the extracted Agency XI folder on your computer.

  • Open the "Figma" folder within the Agency XI folder.

Drag Agency-XI.fig into Figma and Import

  • Launch the Figma desktop or web application on your computer.

  • In the Figma interface, navigate to the "File" menu and select "Import" or simply drag and drop the "Agency-XI.fig" file from the Figma folder into the Figma workspace.

Opening the Agency-XI Project

  • Once the file is imported, locate and click on the "Agency-XI" project within Figma to open it.

Exploring the Template:

  • You're now ready to explore and customize the Agency XI template in Figma.

  • Edit text, colors, images, and other elements to suit your project requirements.

  • Save your changes regularly to ensure your progress is preserved.

Customization Guide

The Agency XI Figma template offers extensive customization options to tailor your digital agency website to your specific needs and preferences. Follow these guidelines to make the most out of the template's flexibility.

Unlock Layers and Hide Grids

  • To unlock frames, you have two options: either click on the lock icon located in the toolbar, or select all frames and press Ctrl + Shift + L on your keyboard.

  • To hide frames, start by selecting the specific frame you wish to hide. Then, click on the eye icon located in the toolbar to conceal the layout grid.

  • Additionally, if you want to toggle the visibility of layout grids for a selected frame, simply press Shift + R on your keyboard. This allows you to quickly show or hide the layout grids as needed.

Text Customization

  • To edit any text, simply double-click on it. Figma provides pre-defined text and heading styles that you can conveniently utilize for your designs. These styles offer consistency and efficiency in formatting text elements across your project. Utilize them to maintain a cohesive visual identity and streamline your design process.

Color Customization

  • Figma offers pre-defined color styles that you can readily apply to your design elements, ensuring consistency and efficiency. If needed, you can modify the default color variables to match your project's requirements. Additionally, you have the option to search for specific color variables to quickly find the ones you need. To customize the default color variable, simply click on the designated area to open the "Edit Color Style" box. From there, you can choose the color you want to use from the available options. This streamlined process allows you to efficiently manage and customize color styles within your design projects.

Image Replacement

  • To add an image to your design, begin by selecting the "Place Image" frame. Ensure that this frame is named "Place Image" for clarity. Confirm that the frame name is indeed "Place Image" to avoid confusion.

  • Once you've selected the "Place Image" frame, proceed to change the image within it. Click on the fill option to initiate this process. Upon clicking, a dialog box will appear on your screen. Within this box, select the "Choose Image" option to navigate to and select the image you wish to use from your device.

  • After selecting the desired image, click on "Open" to finalize your selection and insert the image into the designated frame. This sequence of steps ensures a seamless process for adding images to your design within Figma.

Manage Prototype

  • To enable Prototype mode in Figma, simply click on the "Prototype" tab. Within Prototype mode, you'll notice a blue navigation line that serves as a link between different frames or pages. You can easily drag and drop this blue line to connect it to another page, facilitating seamless navigation within your design.

  • To manage the navigation line, click on it to access options such as editing its properties or deleting it if necessary.

  • Once you've completed creating your prototype and linked the necessary frames, you can preview your prototype by clicking on the play icon. This allows you to simulate the interaction flow and experience how users will navigate through your design.

Save and Share

  • After finalizing your design in Figma, the next step is to share it with your teammates or clients. To do so, click on the "Share" button located in the upper right corner of the Figma interface.

  • After finalizing your design in Figma, the next step is to share it with your teammates or clients. To do so, click on the "Share" button located in the upper right corner of the Figma interface. Once you've clicked on the share button, a dialog box will appear where you can add the email addresses of your teammates or clients. Additionally, you can specify the permissions you want to grant them, such as view-only or edit access.

  • After adding the email addresses and setting the permissions, simply click on the "Invite" button to share your design with them. This action sends an invitation email to the specified recipients, allowing them to access and collaborate on the Figma file according to the permissions you've granted.

Agency XI Resources

Discover the key resources behind Agency XI's design, including Dribbble for inspiration, Coolors for color palettes, Google Fonts for typography, and more. Elevate your website with high-quality assets from Shutterstock, Pixels, and Iconoir icons

Dribbble for Inspiration

Dribbble serves as a platform where designers showcase their work, offering inspiration for creative projects. Please be aware that direct use or reproduction of Dribbble designs may be subject to copyright restrictions.

Coolors for Color Palette

Coolors is a color scheme generator that helps designers create cohesive color palettes for their projects. Use Coolors to explore, create, and effortlessly customize vibrant color palettes that complement your design vision.

Google Fonts for Fonts

Google Fonts offers an extensive library of free, open-source fonts that can be easily integrated into web projects. Choose from a diverse selection of fonts to enhance readability, aesthetics, and versatility in your designs.

Shutterstock for Images

Shutterstock is popular platforms for accessing high-quality stock images and photos. These resources offer a wide range of visual assets to enhance the visual appeal and creativity of your designs.

Iconoir for Icons

Iconoir is a library of open-source icons designed to complement various design projects. With Iconoir, you can easily find and integrate icons into your designs to enhance visual communication and user experience.

Figma Community

The Figma Community is a platform where designers share and discover resources, including templates and design assets. Explore the Figma Community to find additional inspiration for your design projects.
